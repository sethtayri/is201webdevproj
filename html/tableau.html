Tableau Graph

Below is a graph from a recent research project of mine, which you can read more about here. The study demonstrates that when placed with resumes with different types of pronouns, those with they/them pronouns are the least likely to be considered "qualified" for a professional job. The other categories include: resumes with names that match pronouns (e.g. male name with he/him pronouns), resumes with names that do not match pronouns (e.g. male name with she/her pronouns), and resumes with no pronouns listed.
